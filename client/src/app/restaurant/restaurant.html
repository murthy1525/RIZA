Our Story

The journey of RIZA

RIZA was born from a simple dream: to create a place where food brings people together. Founded in 2020, we started as a small family restaurant with a passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

Over the years, we've grown from a neighborhood favorite to a culinary destination, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe in using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, preparing each dish with care, and treating every guest like family.

Our Beginning

Started with a vision to serve authentic, flavorful food in a warm, welcoming environment. What began as a small venture has grown into a beloved community restaurant.

Our Growth

Through dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, RIZA has earned the trust and love of thousands of guests who return time and again.

Our Future

We continue to innovate while staying true to our roots, always striving to exceed expectations and create memorable dining experiences.
